PERSONAL PROJECT

69 Bawtry Road
Sheffield, S9 1UA


Mr. Pham reached out to me to transform a run down and old house to the existing site at 69 Bawtry Road, Sheffield, S9 1UA. The scheme is a semi-detached house with a garage and external works. The planning application wasn't straightforward because there was a concern with the highway and air quality; the planning officer was not convinced that the site's air quality was safe. For the application to move forward, I asked Spire Environmental to conduct air quality testing. After at least six months, I had the green light and go ahead with the planning application. On 12 December 2016, the application was GRANTED (Ref:16/02931/OUT).
